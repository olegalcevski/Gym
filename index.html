Our Story

MORE THAN A GYM.

Nestled in the vibrant neighbourhood of Debar Maalo, right at the heart of Skopje, Pestal Gym is where serious training meets a genuine community. We're not a chain, not a franchise — we're a local institution built on passion, hard work, and the belief that everyone deserves access to a world-class fitness environment.

Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or you're a seasoned athlete looking for a focused training space — Pestal Gym gives you the tools, the atmosphere, and the support to push past your limits every single day.

  • Premium equipment for strength and cardio training
  • Experienced, results-driven coaching staff
  • Central location in Debar Maalo — easy to reach
  • A focused, no-nonsense training atmosphere
  • Real results for real people, every day
